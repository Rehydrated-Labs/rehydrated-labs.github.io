# Marsh

**Marsh** pits are quite light and airy. They tend to be appear in colder biomes.

## Generation

Marshes generate as a large field full of small algae bog pits, with algae grass and weed growing around the edges.

## Sinking

A Marsh is full of long strands of algae and roots that tangle around the player, making escape quite difficult. The player needs to struggle upwards to keep up horizontal movement, but struggling is challenging.
